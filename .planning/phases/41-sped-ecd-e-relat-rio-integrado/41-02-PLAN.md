    Endpoints:
    - `GET ${base}/download?fiscalYearId=xxx&costCenterId=yyy` — calls generateIntegratedReport, sets `Content-Type: application/pdf`, `Content-Disposition: attachment; filename="${filename}"`, sends buffer
