    Find the route handler for `POST ${base}/:id/post` (the "post draft" endpoint). Add `checkPeriodOpen()` as middleware in the handler chain, AFTER authenticate and checkPermission, BEFORE the async handler:

The middleware is defense-in-depth for endpoints that DO receive a date in body.
